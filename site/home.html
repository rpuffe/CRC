Our Story

The Stick Ponies owe their roots to open mic events in Jamestown. After playing together long enough and having too much fun, they began booking gigs and playing shows together.

The Stick Ponies have a distinct sound that separates them from other groups. Covering a variety of songs with a splash of originals, this group is ready to entertain!

If you love having a good time, you’ll love the Stick Ponies.
